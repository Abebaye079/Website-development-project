About Lissan Language School

Lissan online language school was created to make ethiopian languages accessible to learners around the world. Whether you're reconnecting with your roots, learning for travel, or studying for work, we bring expert-led, culturally rich lessons right to your screen.

Explore our courses

Our Mission

At Lissan, our mission is simple: to preserve, celebrate, and teach Ethiopia's diverse languages through accessible, high-quality online education.

We believe that language is more than vocabulary-it's identity, culture, and connection. Our online learning experience focuses on practical speaking, cultural immersion, and real communication skills. Every course is designed to help learners feel confident, supported, and connected to Ethiopia's heritage.
